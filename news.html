2020 American Lung Association Stair Climb

Pictured above Firefighters Corey Pelle, Jonathan Schmidt and Danny Taylor are presented medals for completing the American Lung Association Fight for Air Climb at Carew Tower in Cincinnati. Firefighters raised money for the American Lung Association and participated in the climb while wearing full firefighter protective gear. The climb consisted of climbing 45 floors totaling in 804 steps.

Open Burning

Burn Permits Available From October 1, 2018, thru April 30, 2019, If you need a burn permit:

1. Stop at the Silver Grove or the Eastern Campbell Station Monday through Friday 8 AM to 5 PM, and Saturday 8 to 12 PM to fill out an application to burn and schedule a time to inspect the burn area.

2. You may print a copy of the permit in the form sections and fill in the applicable information. This can be presented at one of the locations above to the Fire Officer responsible for the Burn Area inspection. Burn area inspections must be scheduled in advance of issuing the permit and will be scheduled at the convenience of the officer designated by the Fire Chief to issue permits.

3. Turn in your application for a burn permit at least 48 hours before you plan to burn as burning cannot take place until the burn area is inspected and a permit issued.

-Wayne Werrmann, Fire Chief
